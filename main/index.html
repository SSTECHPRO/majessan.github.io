  • MIG Welding Machine

    Welding with metal inert gas is typically used for large and thick materials. It makes use of a consumable wire that serves as both an electrode and a filler material. It is much faster than TIG welding, resulting in shorter lead times and lower production costs.

    Continue Reading

  • Arc Welding

    Shielded metal arc welding (SMAW), otherwise called manual metal bend welding (MMA or MMAW), motion protected circular segment welding or casually as stick welding, is a manual bend welding process that utilizes a consumable cathode covered with a transition to lay the weld.

    Continue Reading

  • Powder Coating MAchine

    Powder Coating is a dry completing cycle made by an electric charge that makes a dry powder wire to the outer layer of the metal. This is then prepared in a relieving broiler to accomplish a smooth covering. It is generally used to make a hard completion that is harder than customary paint.

    Continue Reading

  • CNC - TPP Punching Machine

    CNC punching is a sheet metal manufacturing process that is performed by CNC punch presses. These machines can be designed with a single head and tool rail or a multi-tool turret.

    Continue Reading

  • shearing Machine

    Shearing machines are multipurpose machines that are used to cut alloys and other sheet metal. Some shearing machines cut metal into sheets or strips using a scissor-like, angular shear action.

    Continue Reading

  • Bending Machine

    A bending machine is a tool for forming (DIN 8586). Its purpose is to put together a bend on a workpiece. A bend is created by bending a tool during a linear or rotating movement.
